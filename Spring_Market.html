Bishop Reding Spring Market Fries on the Go!

"Fast. Fresh. Fries"

By: Dylan Wehrle
Price: 3 Tickets for Fries, 4 Tickets with toppings
Fries are the quintessential comfort food that have been a beloved staple for generations. The crispy, golden exterior and fluffy, soft interior of each fry is simply irresistible, and when seasoned with salt or paired with your favorite dipping sauce, they reach new heights of flavor. Whether as a satisfying snack or a tasty side dish, fries always hit the spot and leave you craving more..
Tomatoes are a staple in many cuisines, and for good reason! They are juicy, tangy, and sweet all at once, with a texture that can range from tender to meaty. Whether used as the star of a dish or simply as a supporting player, tomatoes add depth and complexity to any meal. Sliced onto a sandwich, roasted with herbs and olive oil, or tossed into a fresh salad, tomatoes are a versatile ingredient that can be enjoyed in countless ways. With their bright color and bold flavor, it's no wonder tomatoes are a beloved food around the world.
Guacamole is a rich and creamy avocado-based dip that is beloved by many. The combination of fresh avocados, tangy lime juice, and savory spices makes for a flavorful and irresistible spread that is perfect for dipping tortilla chips, topping tacos, or adding to sandwiches. The smooth and silky texture of guacamole pairs perfectly with crunchy chips or crispy veggies, and the rich flavor of the avocado is perfectly balanced with the bright notes of fresh herbs and citrus. Whether you're a die-hard guac fan or new to the game, there's no denying that a good guacamole is a true delight for the taste buds.
Jalapenos are the perfect addition to any dish for those who love a spicy kick. Their bright, tangy flavor and mild heat make them a versatile ingredient that can be used in many dishes. From topping burgers to adding to salsa, jalapenos add a delicious depth of flavor that elevates any dish. They are also a great source of vitamins A and C, making them a healthy addition to any meal. So next time you want to spice things up, reach for some jalapenos and take your dish to the next level.
Sour cream is a versatile and delicious condiment that adds a tangy and creamy flavor to many dishes. Its smooth texture and slightly tart taste make it the perfect complement to savory foods like tacos, baked potatoes, and chili. Whether dolloped on top of a warm bowl of soup or mixed into a spicy dip, sour cream adds a richness and depth of flavor that is hard to resist. Its cool and refreshing taste also helps to balance out the heat from spicy foods, making it a go-to ingredient for those who love a little kick in their cuisine. Once you try it, you'll understand why sour cream is a beloved ingredient in so many delicious recipes.
Lettuce, also known as the king of salads, is a crunchy and refreshing leafy green that's a staple in many dishes. Its mild flavor and crisp texture make it the perfect base for any salad, sandwich, or wrap. Whether it's the classic iceberg lettuce or the more nutrient-dense romaine or kale, lettuce adds a burst of freshness and crunch to any meal. Plus, it's low in calories and high in vitamins and minerals, making it a guilt-free addition to your diet. So go ahead, pile on the lettuce, and take your meal to the next level!
Cheese is the ultimate comfort food that never fails to satisfy. The creamy, smooth texture and rich, savory flavor of each bite is simply heavenly. Whether melted on a pizza, layered in a sandwich, or paired with crackers and fruit for a snack, cheese is a versatile ingredient that adds a delicious depth of flavor to any dish. With so many varieties to choose from, there's a cheese for every taste preference, making it a beloved staple in every kitchen.
Loaded fries are the ultimate indulgence for any fry lover. The crispy fries topped with gooey melted cheese, savory bacon, tangy green onions, and a dollop of creamy sour cream create a flavor explosion in every bite. The combination of different textures and flavors makes loaded fries the perfect comfort food that satisfies your cravings and leaves you wanting more.
